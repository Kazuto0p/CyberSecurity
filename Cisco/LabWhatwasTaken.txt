### Common lessons learned

Across these incidents, the same themes appear repeatedly:

* **Enable Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)** for all critical systems.
* **Apply security patches quickly** to eliminate known vulnerabilities.
* **Train employees** to recognize phishing and social engineering attacks.
* **Use Endpoint Detection & Response (EDR)** to detect malware and suspicious behavior.
* **Implement Zero Trust and least-privilege access** to limit attacker movement.
* **Maintain encrypted, offline backups** to recover from ransomware.
* **Continuously monitor networks** and conduct regular security assessments.
* **Strengthen third-party/vendor security**, since many breaches begin through suppliers or contractors.
